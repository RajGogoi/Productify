Productify

Productify is a simple task manager app that allows you to record and group tasks together based on projects. Consider this an upgraded version of your traditional to-do list. In addition to recording and organizing your own tasks, you can use the app to use a timer and also schedule tasks using its inbuilt calender.Productify is one of the best tools on the market for collecting all of your ideas and thoughts in one place. Our app is known for its clean and simple interface, which sets it apart from other time-tracking tools and makes it really easy and intuitive for users.
